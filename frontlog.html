About Us

FJA Basketball Court was established in 2022. It is located inside the compound of FJA, which also offers a pavilion and swimming pool. Click here to book at the pavilion or pool!

FJA Basketball Court has two courts: Juliet and Andoy, named after our grandmother Juliet and grandfather Andoy.

Location

Located at 0893 J.P. Rizal St., Sta. Barbara, Baliwag, Bulacan, in front of Iglesia Ni Cristo - Lokal ng Sta. Barbara.
